About Us

  • Why Us
  • Area
Eddie Torr has been working as an exterior cleaner for over 6 years. He is hard working, polite and conscientious. He has a passion for cleaning and always does it with a smile on his face. He likes a cup of tea with one sugar!
Eagle Eyed Exterior Cleaning offers a professional service for all of your residential and commercial outside cleaning needs including windows with frames and sills as standard, facias and gutters, conservatories, gutter clearing, carports, cladding, driveways, patios and decking. We use pure water but can also clean by hand if required.
Covering the Nottinghamshire and Loughborough areas including Rushcliffe, Newark and Sherwood, Broxtowe, Gedling and Nottingham City. If you live outside of this area, please still contact us for free quote
